üìå Registration

  1. Go to the registration page, where you need to fill in the required fields.
  2. If the email is not already registered, you will be able to sign up.

üîë Login

  1. Login with either email/password or Google.
  2. If you choose to log in with Google, a modal will open where you need to select your Google account.
  3. If your Google email is already registered, it will ask if you want to combine the accounts.

üìã Main Tabs

  • The app has 5 tabs: Home, Courses, Classy IA, Profile, Configuration.

Home

  • In the Home tab, you can see the roles you can have within the app.

Courses

  • You will see your courses divided into 5 sections: My Courses, Public Courses, Enrolled Courses, and Favorites.
  • To create a new course, click the "+" button at the bottom right of the screen.

Course Details

  • If you are a professor, you will be able to view and edit the course details.
  • In the "Modules" section, you can see a list of all modules, which can be reordered as needed.

Module Details

  • In each module, you can view detailed information and a list of resources that can also be reordered.
  • Click the "+" button to add a resource (image, video, or external link).

Assessment

  • In the "Assessment" section, you will find a list of all assessments (exams or tasks) that you can filter.
  • To create a new assessment, click the "+" button at the bottom right of the screen.
  • Each exercise can be of type "Open" or "Multiple Choice".

Exercise View

  • As a teacher, you can view each exercise's details, edit them, and view student submissions.
  • You can also export the assessment as a PDF.

Feedback

  • In the feedback section, you can view feedback for the course, provide feedback to students, and view AI-generated course feedback.

Statistics

  • In the "Course Stats" section, you can see the course performance statistics.
  • You can also view statistics for individual students and assessments.
  • Statistics can be exported as a PDF.

Configuration

  • You can log out or configure your notifications in the Configuration section.
